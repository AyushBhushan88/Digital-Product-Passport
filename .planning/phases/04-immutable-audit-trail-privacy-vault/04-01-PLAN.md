Implement a cryptographic audit trail for ingestion payloads using SHA-256 hashing and an AuditLog model.

Purpose: Establish data integrity and auditability as per EU DPP standards (D-13, D-14).
Output: A persistent audit log with cryptographic hashes for every ingestion event.
</objective>
